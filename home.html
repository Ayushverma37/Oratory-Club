About us

Enarrators was formed in 2019 by Taneesh Kaushik. We are one of the most active and disciplined club in IIT Ropar. We hold several events including JAM, Turncoat, Speeches, etc. We aim at improving ones public speaking skills and boosting confidence. We also have lot of fun here.
